---
title: "Setting business details in partner payout invoices"
description: "Learn how to set custom business details (company name, address, tax ID) in your partner payout invoices."
sidebarTitle: "Customizing invoice details"
---

Every time you [receive a payout](/help/article/receiving-payouts) on Dub, our platform automatically generates an invoice for accounting purposes.

With Dub, you can also set your business details (company name, address, tax ID) in your payout invoices for tax compliance.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the payout settings modal">

First, go to your [payout settings page](https://partners.dub.co/settings/payouts) and click on the **Payout settings** button:

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/payout-settings-button.png"
    alt="partner invoice settings button"
  />
</Frame>

</Step>
<Step title="Enter your business details">
This will open up the Payout settings modal, where you can set your business name, address, and tax ID:

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/payout-settings-fields.png"
    alt="partner invoice settings"
  />
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step title="Save changes and view updated invoice">

Once the changes are saved, they are automatically reflected in your payout invoices:

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/partner-invoice-example.png"
    alt="partner invoice example"
  />
</Frame>

</Step>
</Steps>
