First, [create a new workspace](/help/article/what-is-a-workspace#how-to-create-a-workspace) or navigate to an existing workspace.

Go to your workspace settings page by clicking on the **Settings** tab in the nav bar.

<img src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/workspace-id-settings.png" alt="The Settings link in your Dub workspace" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100" />

Then, scroll down to the **Workspace ID** section on the General page to find your Workspace ID.

<img src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/workspace-id-location.png" alt="The Workspace ID section in your Dub workspace settings" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100" />
