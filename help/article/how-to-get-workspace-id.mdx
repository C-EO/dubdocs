---
title: "How to get my Workspace ID on Dub?"
description: "This article will guide you on how to get your Workspace ID on Dub."
---

First, [create a new workspace](/help/article/what-is-a-workspace#how-to-create-a-workspace) or navigate to an existing workspace.

Go to your workspace settings page by clicking on the **Settings** tab in the nav bar.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/workspace-id-settings.png"
    alt="The Settings link in your Dub workspace"
  />
</Frame>

Then, scroll down to the **Workspace ID** section on the General page to find your Workspace ID.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="https://assets.dub.co/cms/workspace-id-location.png"
    alt="The Workspace ID section in your Dub workspace settings"
  />
</Frame>
