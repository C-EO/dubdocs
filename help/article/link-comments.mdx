On Dub.co, you can add comments to your links by using the "Comments" section in the link builder.

<img src="https://assets.dub.co/help/comments-section.png" alt="Adding comments to your links in the Dub.co link builder" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100" />

<Info>
  Note: Hyperlinks are supported in comments. You can use this to add more
  context to your links.
</Info>

When you add a comment to your link, it will be visible to you and your team as a
message bubble on the link card.

<img src="https://assets.dub.co/changelog/link-comments.png" alt="Comments are visible as message bubbles on the link card" className="rounded-lg border border-gray-100" />
